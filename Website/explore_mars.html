Curiosity Rover

Curiosity is a car-sized Mars rover designed to explore the Gale crater on Mars as part of NASA's Mars Science Laboratory mission. Curiosity was launched from Cape Canaveral on 26 November 2011 and landed on Mars on 6 August 2012. On 6 August 2022, a detailed overview of accomplishments by the Curiosity rover for the last ten years was reported. The rover is still operational, and as of These Day, Curiosity has been active on Mars for 10 years since its landing.
